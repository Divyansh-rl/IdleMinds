How to Play

Sudoku

The classic logic-based number puzzle. Your goal is to completely fill the 9x9 grid with digits.

  • Rule 1: Every horizontal row must contain the numbers 1-9 exactly once.
  • Rule 2: Every vertical column must contain the numbers 1-9 exactly once.
  • Rule 3: Every bold 3x3 sub-grid must contain the numbers 1-9 exactly once.
  • Pro-Tip: Never guess! Always use logic to find where a number must go based on the numbers already on the board.

Pips

A tricky game of dice and logic. Your goal is to place pairs of dice onto the board to satisfy all given conditions.

  • Rule 1: Analyze the specific number or mathematical conditions listed on the board's blocks.
  • Rule 2: Drag and drop your available pairs of dice into the blocks.
  • Rule 3: The pips (dots) on the dice must satisfy the conditions of the block they are placed in.
  • Rule 4: You win when every pair of dice is placed and all board conditions are simultaneously met!

Zips

A spatial sequencing puzzle. Connect the numbers in order while leaving no trace behind.

  • Rule 1: Start at the number 1 and trace a continuous path to the number 5 in sequential order (1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5).
  • Rule 2: Your path must pass through every single empty block on the screen.
  • Rule 3: The path cannot cross itself, overlap, or move diagonally.
  • Pro-Tip: Plan ahead! If you reach 5 but there are still empty blocks left on the grid, you must backtrack and find a longer route.

Wordcraft

Test your vocabulary. Guess the hidden 5-letter word within your limited attempts.

  • Rule 1: Type any valid 5-letter word and submit your guess.
  • Green Tiles: The letter is in the word and in the exact correct spot.
  • Yellow Tiles: The letter is in the word, but in the wrong spot. Move it somewhere else!
  • Gray Tiles: The letter is not in the hidden word at all.

Connections

A game of lateral thinking. Group a grid of 16 random words into 4 distinct categories.

  • Rule 1: Look at the 16 cards and find 4 words that share a common theme (e.g., "Types of Fish" or "Words that start with 'C'").
  • Rule 2: Select those 4 cards and submit to see if they are a match.
  • Rule 3: Successfully find all 4 groups of 4 to clear the board and win.
  • Pro-Tip: Watch out for red herrings! Some words might look like they belong to multiple categories just to trick you.
